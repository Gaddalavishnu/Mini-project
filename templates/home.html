Welcome to Smart Home Temperature

About

Smart Wi-Fi thermostats have moved well beyond the function they were originally designed for;
namely, controlling heating and cooling comfort in buildings.

They are now also learning from occupant behaviors and permit occupants to control their comfort remotely.

This project seeks to go beyond this state of the art by utilizing smart Wi-Fi thermostat data in residences to develop dynamic predictive models for room temperature and energy use.
